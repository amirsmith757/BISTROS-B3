Our Chef: Nadia Wilson


Nadia was born in Virginia Beach, VA. She was raised in Miami, FL and attended the Miami Culinary Institute.

If she’s not at Bistros, you’ll find her cheering on the Miami Heat or spending time with her dog, Pluto. She considers Jennifer Daniels her hero and her mother to be her greatest inspiration.

Nadia has been working in the restaurant industry for a very long time and really enjoys the environment Bistros offers.
